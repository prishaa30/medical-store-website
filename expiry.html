📚 Expiry Management Guidelines

What to do with expired medicines?

As per Indian Drug & Cosmetics Act:

  • Remove expired medicines from the shelf immediately
  • Store separately in a marked “Expired” area
  • Contact the distributor for return/credit within 30 days
  • Maintain a register of expired medicines for audit
  • Dispose as per local pharmacy guidelines
How to claim credit for expired stock?
  • Contact the distributor or company representative
  • Keep the original invoice and batch details ready
  • Most companies accept returns up to 6 months post-expiry
  • Credit notes are usually issued within 30-60 days
  • Maintain a copy of all return receipts
FIFO Method — First Expiry, First Out

Always sell medicines with the nearest expiry date first. When restocking shelves:

  • Place newer stock at the back
  • Move older stock to the front
  • Check expiry dates weekly for fast-moving items
  • Check expiry dates monthly for slow-moving items
